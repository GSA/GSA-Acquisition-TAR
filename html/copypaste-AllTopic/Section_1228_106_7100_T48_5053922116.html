1228.106-7100 Waiver.

Pursuant to the authority vested in the Secretary of Transportation by the Bond statute at 40 U.S.C. chapter 31, subchapter III, Bonds (historically known as the Miller Act), the requirements of 40 U.S.C. 3131 et seq. are waived, to the extent authorized in accordance with 40 U.S.C. 3134(b).
