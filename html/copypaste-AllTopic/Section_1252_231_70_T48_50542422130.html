1252.231-70 Date of incurrence of costs.

As prescribed in (TAR) 48 CFR 1231.205-32(b), insert the following clause:

Date of Incurrence of Costs (OCT 1994)

The Contractor shall be entitled to reimbursement for costs incurred on or after ____________ in an amount not to exceed $____________ that, if incurred after this contract had been entered into, would have been reimbursable under this contract.

(End of clause)
