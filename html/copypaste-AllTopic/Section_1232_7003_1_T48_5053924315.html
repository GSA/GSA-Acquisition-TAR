1232.7003-1 Electronic authentication.

Access to DELPHI is granted with electronic authentication of credentials (name & valid email address) utilizing the General Services Administration (GSA) credentialing platform login.gov. Vendors submitting invoices will be required to submit invoices via iSupplier (DELPHI) and authenticated via www.login.gov.
