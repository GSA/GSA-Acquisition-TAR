Subpart 1204.1 - Contract Execution

Parent topic: PART 1204 - ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

1204.103 Contract clause.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause at (FAR) 48 CFR 52.204-1, Approval of Contract, filled in as appropriate, in solicitations and contracts when approval to award the resulting contract must be obtained from an official at a level above the contracting officer.
