Subpart 1205.1 - Dissemination of Information

Parent topic: PART 1205 - PUBLICIZING CONTRACT ACTIONS

1205.101 Methods of disseminating information.

(a) The DOT Office of Small and Disadvantaged Business Utilization, 1200 New Jersey Avenue SE, Washington, DC 20590 publishes a Procurement Forecast of planned procurements each fiscal year on their website at https://www.transportation.gov/osdbu/procurement-forecast/summary/.
